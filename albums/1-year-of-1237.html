1 year of 1237

my first anniversary album & my biggest album in 2021. this album was released on September 12th, 2021, exactly 1 year after the release of "0", my first release (which was released on September 12th 2020). this album contains 30 new, original track recorded between June 2021 and September 2021. (and yes it's not a compilation album).

all the track titles in this album represents the duration of the track. ' = minutes and " = seconds. the first track is titled 4'07" because its duration is 4 minutes and 7 seconds, the second track is titled 3'23" because its duration is 3 minutes and 23 seconds, and so on (i should stop naming my songs using this format).

this album has 4 sides, each side contains 7 to 8 tracksand those sides were just added a few hours before i wrote this paragraph.

(most of) the music style of a & b side tracks are pretty similar to tracks from 12:4:8 and Dimensi 7. but the last 3 tracks from side b are chiptune tracks -- i haven't made any chiptune music for a long while, so (when i made this album) i decided to make chiptunes again and then i put those chiptune tracks in the album.

c side is the side that has the most chiptune tracks. yeah, i really like chiptunes, but there are (still) some non-chiptune tracks (those tracks were made when i got tired of making chiptunes).

most of the tracks from the d side are tracks from a failed/discontinued album. the album was planned to release the on August/September 2021, but then i decided to move some of the tracks from that album to 1 year of 1237. there are 9 tracks on the failed album, but only 6 tracks were ""moved"" to 1 year of 1237. i accidentally deleted the other 3 tracks. why did i move all the tracks from the failed album to 1 year of 1237? i don't know
